RulesCompare allows you to cross-compare the text of specific rules across House rules packages. To use the tool, select the Congresses you’d like to compare. Then, in the drop-down menu, select the specific rule you’d like to study. Click “Compare,” and the comparison will appear below.

Additional features let you do the following: disable the diff, enable a gpt generated explanation of rules changes, and selection between comparing full rules and subsections of the rules.

*Because of the rules consolidation between the 105th and 106th Congresses, it is not suggested to compare pre- and post-105th rules. Be wary if you decide to do so.
